01 · Thesis

A first-of-its-kind tax-exempt bond structure to rebuild Pacific Palisades, and a first-mover platform for Six Peak

Uplifters Foundation is a California 501(c)(3) standing up a ~$200M, ~60-home mission-driven rebuild for families displaced by the January 2025 fire. Six Peak would serve as exclusive development manager and owner's representative on a fee-based basis, with no equity and no completion guarantee.

The shape of the engagement

If the model works in the Palisades, it becomes a replicable platform for disaster recovery and workforce housing nationwide.

Six Peak's exposure is fee-for-services. No equity, no completion guarantee, no ownership risk. The structural innovation sits with the Foundation: tax-exempt municipal bond financing to fund market-rate, for-sale single-family housing, with no developer promote and proceeds funding community infrastructure rather than shareholder returns.

02 · Opportunity

Why this engagement, and why now

The Palisades Fire created an urgent rebuilding need that conventional private development can't serve at the scale or pricing the displaced community needs. Uplifters' tax-exempt bond structure threads that needle.

The Foundation

Uplifters Foundation is a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to rebuilding for the most vulnerable victims of the Palisades Fire. Completed homes are first offered at a discount to former residents who commit to return. Net proceeds fund public facilities (playgrounds, parks, utilities) instead of a developer promote.

The Financing Innovation

This is the first known use of tax-exempt municipal bond financing to fund market-rate, for-sale single-family housing at scale. Eliminating the cost of equity capital and the developer promote drives a structural cost advantage versus conventional private builders.

Platform Opportunity Beyond the Palisades

If the program executes successfully, Six Peak becomes the only operator in the country with a demonstrated track record on this financing and delivery model. Three replication paths sit one rung below today's mandate:

  • Adjacent disaster markets: Altadena is the immediate next target. A fundraise effort will commence at the close of this first $200M round. Future climate-driven disasters around the country are the natural extension. The program is a repeatable financial strategy more than a development strategy.
  • Workforce / attainable housing: below-market-rate, new-construction homes are the most desirable form of residential real estate, with a near-endless pool of buyers.
  • Institutional adoption: once one bond closes successfully, the template becomes underwriteable by a much broader pool.
03 · Mandate

What Six Peak is being asked to do

End-to-end development management and owner's representative services from site sourcing through final sale, organized into five service categories. Six Peak acts in a representative and managerial capacity, not as principal, contractor, broker, architect, or engineer.

A
Program-Level

Standards, roster, controls

Build the program-wide playbook: product standards, design-build roster, master agreements, budgets, schedules, reporting formats.

B
Acquisition

Sourcing & predevelopment

Source on- and off-market lots within the buy box; manage diligence (title, zoning, geotech, environmental); oversee close.

C
Project Mgmt

Execution & controls

Design development, value engineering, permitting, construction oversight, draws, change orders, contingency, closeout.

D
Sales & Exit

Lease-to-sale & disposition

Pricing recommendations, broker management, marketing, escrow & contract administration, transaction closing.

E
Post-Completion

Warranty & reconciliation

Warranty claims, punch-list follow-up, final cost reconciliation, release of retained contingency.

Risk profile: The engagement is a fee-based services arrangement, not a principal-risk investment. Six Peak does not contribute equity, assume a completion guarantee, or take ownership risk.

07 · Risk & Deal Status

What we're watching, and where the deal stands

Probability of close is currently estimated at 50/50. The two gating variables are regulatory completion and bond fundraising. Risk profile is execution-shaped, not principal.

Gating Variables & Credit Profile

  • Regulatory pathway. Foundation is progressing through required regulatory and local governance approvals. Process is moving smoothly at California public-agency cadence; expected to clear in the next several weeks.
  • Fundraising. $21M in early written commitments secured in initial outreach. Formal bond issuance and full subscription remain ahead. Target buyers are HNW former Palisades residents redirecting tax-exempt fixed-income allocations.
  • Credit profile. $200M of debt supported by ~$198M of hard assets (land, CIP, capitalized interest), with repayment from rent and home sale proceeds.

08 · Strategic Upside

Why this engagement matters beyond the fee

Three strategic benefits to Six Peak that don't show up in the $9.0M / $4.1M numbers.

Profile & Positioning

Sits Six Peak at the center of one of the highest-profile rebuilding efforts in Los Angeles. Strengthens relationships with institutional capital, municipalities, public agencies, and local stakeholders in ways conventional development assignments rarely do.

Direct Capability Match

Not a passive advisory role. This is a full execution mandate built on the firm's actual operating strengths: site sourcing, underwriting, design-build management, construction oversight, draw administration, disposition.

First-Mover Position

If the program succeeds, Six Peak is the only operator with a proven track record on this financing & delivery model. The relationships, learnings, and operational playbook built in the Palisades become a durable moat.

How to read the strategic upside: The $4.1M of net income is the floor of what this engagement is worth to Six Peak. It captures the contractual revenue but not the option value of being the only operator in the country with a proven track record on this model when the next disaster market or workforce-housing program comes calling.
